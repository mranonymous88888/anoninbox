Rules & Privacy Policy

At Anon In Box, we are committed to providing a secure and private email service that respects the confidentiality and anonymity of our users. Our primary goal is to create a safe and trustworthy environment where individuals can communicate freely, without fear of surveillance or data exploitation. In order to achieve this, we have established a set of rules and guidelines that all users must adhere to, as well as a comprehensive privacy policy that outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect it.

Prohibited Activities

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM): We have a zero-tolerance policy towards the creation, dissemination, or possession of CSAM. Any user found to be engaging in such activities will be immediately reported to the relevant authorities and banned from our platform.
  • Zoophilia: The exploitation or abuse of animals for sexual purposes is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination.
  • Necrophilia: The promotion or glorification of necrophilia is not allowed on our platform and will be met with swift action.
  • Human Trafficking: We do not permit the use of our platform for human trafficking, including the recruitment, transportation, or exploitation of individuals for labor or sexual purposes.
  • Illegal Substances: The sale, promotion, or distribution of illegal substances, including narcotics, is strictly prohibited and will result in account termination.

Data Collection and Storage

At Anon In Box, we are committed to minimizing the amount of data we collect and store. We only collect the following information:

  • Username: Your chosen username, which will be used to identify your account.
  • Email Address: Your email address, which will be used to send and receive emails.
  • Password: Your password, which will be used to secure your account.

Data Protection and Confidentiality

We do not share data unless legally required. User anonymity is a priority.
